Scenario 01: Bank Fraud Detection

The Time & Distance Anomaly

Under traditional descriptive analysis, card audits happen 30 days after the month ends. The money is lost. Under next-generation streaming systems, a spatial-temporal velocity equation immediately runs as transactions occur.

Descriptive (Old)

Reconciliation Audit

Found monthly. Cardholder loses money. Bank refunds via insurance weeks later.

Next-Gen (Real-Time)

Velocity Audit

Triggers automatically during swipes. Lockouts occur instantly before charge succeeds.
